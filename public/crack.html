How it works

HashWizard maintains a Precomputed Hash Table of over 50 Million words, it then breaks them down into chunks and searches through each one of them to retrieve your query. This process is not perfect, the table may not be able to crack long sentences or long series of integers, It is specifically optimized for cracking passwords or english words
